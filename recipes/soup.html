Ingredients

  • 500g Sweet Potato
  • 2-3 Carrots (medium)
  • 3cm Ginger Root
  • 2dl Cream
  • Water
  • Ground pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Chop the sweet potato and carrots and place them in the pot
  2. Fill the pot with water until the ingridients are covered
  3. Let it boil until vegetable are soft
  4. Puree the veggies
  5. Grate the ginger and add it to the pot alonside the cream
  6. Bring to boil and season to taste
